DISCOVER BUDDHISM
    Beginner level
    Directed by: Ven. Norbu

What is Discover Buddhism?

It is a study program of approximately two years duration, which offers a structured training in the fundamental aspects of Buddhist meditation, philosophy and practice. This program is from a Western and secular perspective. This program is designed to savour the experience of meditation and the teachings of the Buddha and in this way positively transform our lives. It offers a program of teachings, readings, texts, meditations, intensive days of practice and evaluations. The importance of an ethical code of conduct and the development of a compassionate attitude towards others is emphasized.

    Who is it for:
To people who want to enrich themselves through the techniques of mental transformation offered by this ancient doctrine, as well as for all those who want to acquire a greater knowledge of meditation, Buddhist philosophy and tradition from an open and western point of view. No prior knowledge is necessary.
    What is recorded
From a 14-module agenda featuring fundamental themes of Buddhist doctrine. Each module is complemented by a meditation retreat program to deepen and integrate the exposed teachings.
    Program Modules

1.- The mind and its potential

2.- How to meditate

3.- Presentation of the path.

4.- Death and rebirth

5.- All about karma

6.- Samsara and Nirvana

7.- Transform problems

8.- How to develop Bodhicitta

9.- The wisdom of emptiness

10.- Shelter in the three jewels

11.- How to establish a daily practice

12.- The spiritual master

13.- Introduction to tantra

14.- Pilgrimage to sacred places

15.- Integration practices

    The mind and its potential
What is the "mind", its nature and functioning, and how it affects our experience of happiness and suffering. Explore the difference between mind and brain, the mind as the creator of our experiences. Learn methods to transform destructive thoughts and cultivate positive attitudes.
    How to meditate
Basic meditation techniques. Learn how to meditate and the purpose of meditation, how to sit properly, how to organize a meditation session, the various types of meditation techniques, and how to recognize and remove obstacles to meditation.
    Presentation of the path
Introduction to the basic principles of the Path to Enlightenment. Learn about the life of Buddha and the basic teachings of Buddhism. Discover the unique system of Buddhist philosophy contained in the "Lam-Rim" or Gradual Path to Awakening.
    Death and rebirth
Reflect on the meaning of death and what to expect at the time of death. Prepare for that decisive moment. Become aware of the ephemeral nature of life in order to live it more meaningfully. Explore the process of death and rebirth and its impact on how we live our lives.
    All about karma
Learn the basics of the law of cause and effect, to gain a clear understanding of how karma works. Discover effective tools for accumulating merit - the cause of happiness and success - and for purifying negative actions done in the past. Explore effective ways to live a meaningful life and take control of this and future lives.
    Samsara and Nirvana
Find out what "samsara" is and how we stick to it. Find out what "nirvana" is and how to achieve it. Develop practical tools to try to eliminate the emotions that disturb us to be free from suffering
    Transforming problems
Learning from problems in order to generate compassion, love, and other qualities on the spiritual path. Reflecting on the disadvantages of selfishness and the advantages of benefiting others. Cultivating compassionate courage "to exchange oneself for others," and learning special techniques of mind training or "Lo-Jong" as a means of transforming problems into happiness.
    How to develop bodhicitta
Discover the meditation method offered by Tibetan Buddhism to develop our natural qualities of kindness and compassion. Become an expert in the application of these techniques to generate the mind of bodhichitta, the desire to achieve enlightenment for the benefit of others.
    Wisdom of emptiness
The realization of emptiness is crucial for achieving liberation and enlightenment. Take this opportunity to enhance your ability to achieve this realization. Learn how to develop it to live in peace and various methods to use in emptiness meditation. Practice the accumulation of merit and the purification of obstacles - essential to generate realizations within the mind.
    Shelter in the three jewels
Learn the meaning of taking refuge in The Three Jewels (Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha) and the essential practices of the Buddhist refuge. Discover the benefits of taking vows and their role in our spiritual growth.
    Establish a daily practice
Apply the tools you need to develop correct daily practice. With The Daily Practice of Meditation, familiarize yourself with the elements necessary to generate realizations in the mind. Get some guides to make every daily action more meaningful.
    The spiritual master
The need for a spiritual master, qualities of the disciple and the disciple, how to relate to the spiritual master, what to do when problems appear and how to meditate to develop devotion to the Master.
    Introduction to Tantra
Learn the definition of tantra, how tantra is worked and why it is a great method of practice. Get a comprehensive description of the four tantra classes and learn how to practice Kriya tantra methods. Further, Find out how to integrate tantra practices with Lam-rim meditation for optimal results.
    Integration practices
Undertaking intensive Lam-rim purification and withdrawal practices, prepare your mind in the best possible way to gain achievement on the way to enlightenment. Purification practices include: 100,000 prostrations, three-month withdrawal from Vajrasattva and Nyung Ne. This is a great way to seal the blessings of this program with the final module of Experiences.
"Use problems as ornaments, seeing them as extremely precious, because they make you attain enlightenment quickly, by making you attain bodhichitta. Experience these problems on behalf of all sentient beings, giving all happiness to sentient beings. This is the ornament." "Lama Zopa Rinpoche"
