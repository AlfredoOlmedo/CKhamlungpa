FUNDAMENTAL PILLARS


FPMT

The FPMT represents a seasoned philosophical and meditative tradition with long experience and a solid foundation in a widely contrasted tradition.



Buddhism

One of the two main branches of Buddhism and a term for the classification of Buddhist philosophies and practices. The term "Mahāyāna" also refers to the path of the bodhisattva who seeks complete enlightenment for the benefit of all beings.



Meditation

Meditation is the main tool that Buddhism proposes to stabilize the mind, achieve calm and transform what we feel and think, a knowledge that we have been developing for 2,600 years.
