    LEVEL: Beginner

    Hatha is a practice that unites your mind and body, connecting the energy that flows from the inside out. It focuses on the optimal execution of postures for each type of person and that provides the greatest benefit.

    In Hatha Yoga we seek to have a practice with full awareness of breathing and postures and achieve the benefits according to your abilities.

    "Hatha" is part of the eight branches of Yoga that speaks of the balance of two internal forces: breathing and exhalation, as well as the mind and the body.
