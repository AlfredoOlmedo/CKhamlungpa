THE DEBATE IN TIBETAN BUDDHISM
    LEVEL: Beginner
    DATES: February 2, 9, 16, 23 and March 2, 9, 16, 23 and 30 (9 sessions in total)
    SCHEDULE: from 20:00 p.m. to 21:30 p.m.
    DIRECTED BY: Geshe Lobsang Dawa
    MODALITY: Online and face-to-face with advance reservation "We have limited space".


The purpose of this course is to learn the Tibetan Buddhist debate technique, a powerful tool to better understand reality through the use of logic, which is part of the traditional training of Tibetan Buddhist monks in great monasteries. The debate is a great ally to notice the logical inconsistencies in the reasoning of others and oneself, thus allowing us to move towards a more consistent understanding and behavior.

Please reserve your place via inbox on social networks or on WhatsApp at 33 3183 7484


Temary:



  • How is existence defined and what are its divisions.

  • Permanent and impermanent phenomena.

  • The importance of definitions and divisions of phenomena.

  • Definiendum and definiens.

  • The different relationships that can exist between two phenomena.

  • Structure of the reasoning in the debate.

  • Reductio ad absurdum in the debate.

  • Questions and possible answers in the debate.

  • The eight doors of implication.

  • Transformation of thought by the technique of debate.

  • Body language symbolism in debate.

  • Discussion exercises.
