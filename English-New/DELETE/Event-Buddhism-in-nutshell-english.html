BUDDHISM IN A NUTSHELL
    LEVEL: Beginner
    DATES: February 12, 19, 26 and March 5
    4 Session course
    SCHEDULE: from 11:00 a.m. to 13:00 p.m.
    DIRECTORS: Dimitri Limberopulos


Buddhism in a few words is the initial level within the regular study programs that the FPMT has designed to have a gradual approach to the philosophy and practice of Tibetan Buddhism.

This course introduces participants to the most basic and important aspects of Buddhist philosophy, psychology, and precepts, as well as meditation techniques and methods. It is a theoretical and practical course, with a participatory tone, in which talks, meditation and debate alternate.

If you have ever been interested in this philosophy or are simply curious to learn a little more, this workshop is the one for you because you will discover effective tools to work with your interior.
