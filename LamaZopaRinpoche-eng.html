MASTERS
Kyabje Lama Thubten Zopa Rinpoche
Lama Thubten Zopa Rinpoche (1946 - 2023), Solu Khumbu (Nepal), at the age of three was recognized as the reincarnation of Lawudo Lama, who had lived nearby.

At age ten, Rinpoche went to Tibet, where he remained until the Chinese occupation of Tibet in 1959 forced him into exile in India, where he met Lama Yeshe.

The Lamas went to Nepal in 1967 and began teaching Dharma to Westerners.

In 1974, they began traveling around the world to teach and establish Dharma centers. When Lama Yeshe passed away in 1984, Rinpoche took over as spiritual leader of his organization, the Foundation for the Preservation of the Mahayana Tradition (FPMT), which continues to flourish under his unparalleled leadership.
